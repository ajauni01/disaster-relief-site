Current Events

Our disaster relief group provides assistance for the surrounding communities when situations arise.

We provide assistance to the following:

  • Floods
  • Tornadoes
  • Fires
  • Blizzards

When we are called to help, the following groups assist:

  • Fire Department
  • Law Enforcement
  • EMS / Hospitals
  • Army National Guard
  • Department of Roads
  • Power Companies
  • Phone / Communication Companies
  • Volunteer Groups

    • Note: Volunteer groups and civilians can sign up to help with disaster relief.
